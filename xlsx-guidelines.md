# Spreadsheet Guidelines

Use the workbook layout, styling, and formula conventions that the skill expects:

- keep the styled comparison sheet
- keep the summary sheet
- preserve direct product links in the link columns
- use timestamped filenames with date and time
- keep formula cells visible and readable in the generated workbook
- keep the run status and reference-output rules in the summary sheet

If an earlier workbook already passed the success gate, use it as the reference for styling and structure.
